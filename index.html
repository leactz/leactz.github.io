Grateful Heart Design.

Grateful Heart Design is my one-woman graphic design and illustration operation. I am currently also a student front-end developer. I am excited about the opportunity to provide my clients with the most well-rounded design service possible: helping to sculpt their business identity and presence at all levels. Every client is unique and I approach each project with empathy, honest design guidance, and a transparent workflow.

I am absolutely passionate about helping people realize their vision. Whether you are campaigning, doing a small project on the side, starting your very own business or revamping your existing corporate identity - I will be happy to help.

The list provides a short roundup of my skills.

  • Vector design, photo manipulation, vector and raster illustration

  • Logo design, mascot design, visual assets, layout, home and office decor

  • Professional efficiency in industry-standard media development software

  • Professional efficiency in the technical aspects of correct design output - for example, print vs web (as well as the many sub-categories)

  • Growing knowledge of web development, with a focus on innovative and responsive mobile-friendly design
